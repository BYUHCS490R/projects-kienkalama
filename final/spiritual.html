What are S.M.A.R.T. goals?

Specific Measurable Attainable Realistic Timely
Specific Your goal should be clear and define what specifically you want to achieve
Measurable The goal that you make should be able to be tracked and should define how you success is measured
Attainable Can you achieve your goal in the amount of time that you have or with the resouces that are available to you?
Relevant If you succeed in that goal will it help will it help you in other aspects of your life?
Timely What is the time table for your goals? How fast do you want to be able to achieve them?
